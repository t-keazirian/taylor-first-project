How did I get into coding, you ask?

My career for the past 10+ years has been in hospitality and restaurant management. I thrive in a fast-paced, driven work environment, and loved working my way up from server all the way to Assistant General Manager. My ability to be detail-oriented, goal-focused, and highly organized allowed me to excel in this field. However, over the past year I found myself yearning for something more - a field where I could keep my mind sharp, and a career that would challenge me in new, exciting ways.

I grew up playing on computers since I could read, as my dad was involved in the early tech industry. As I pondered my options for future careers, software engineering continually popped into my mind. I knew it would be extremely challenging, but also rewarding. I enrolled at Thinkful and never looked back!
